I really enjoyed my years living at The Marshall while attending the University of Minnesota. The amenities made daily life so convenient, and I especially appreciated the mail room — I never had to worry about the security of my packages, which is such a relief as a student constantly ordering books, supplies, or the occasional treat.

The leasing manager, Karli, was a standout during my time there. She was always pleasant, professional, and made sure I was well taken care of, even while juggling a busy office and the needs of other residents.

Overall, I highly recommend The Marshall as a great place to live (and study!) for anyone attending the U. It was more than just student housing — it really felt like a home as I made so many friends there!!!
